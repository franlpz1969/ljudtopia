ABOUT US

We are a spanish-swedish architectural office based in Madrid, dedicated to both new construction and renovation architectural projects, specialized in construction systems and building acoustics, which works under the highest international quality standards to improve people's living conditions.

Stress-free property projects in Spain: From planning permits to solving neighbor noise issues, we handle it all in English or Swedish.

Our lead architect is fluent in English, Swedish, and Spanish, with deep knowledge of spanish national and regional regulations. We help international clients to build, renovate, or silence noisy neighbors without the bureaucracy headache.

ACOUSTIC SOLUTIONS

Noise Complaint? We can help with professional acoustic solutions for residential and commercial spaces.

Professional Acoustic Services

  • Noise assessments and measurements (neighbors, traffic, commercial premises)
  • Soundproofing design and installation for homes, studios, or commercial spaces
  • Acoustic conditioning for optimal sound quality (restaurants, offices, home theaters)
  • Legal support for noise disputes (expert reports for lawsuits or mediation)

Specialized Acoustic Solutions

  • Impact noise reduction (footsteps from upstairs neighbors)
  • Airborne noise insulation (voices, TV, music through walls)
  • Facade acoustic insulation (street noise, traffic)
  • Mechanical equipment noise control (HVAC, elevators, plumbing)
  • Room acoustics optimization (reverberation control, echo reduction)

With over 150 successful acoustic projects in our portfolio, we offer the widest range of solutions to match both your requirements and budget.
