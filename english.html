GUIDE FOR BUYING, RENOVATING OR BUILDING IN SPAIN

1. BUYING – We help you evaluate the property technically and legally before purchasing.

2. RENOVATION – Full project design and coordination with Spanish standards.

3. NEW CONSTRUCTION – From idea to turnkey solution.

4. PERMITS AND BUREAUCRACY – We assist with local procedures, documents, and translation.

GUIDE: URBAN AND BUILDING PERMITS IN SPAIN

1. PLANNING STATUS – Understand what is allowed and how early to act.

2. MINOR WORKS LICENSE – For small-scale renovations (e.g., bathrooms).

3. MAJOR WORKS LICENSE – For structural changes or change of use.

4. OTHER REQUIREMENTS – Certificates, occupation licenses, safety plans.

GUIDE: ACOUSTIC INSULATION IN HOMES AND COMMERCIAL PREMISES

1. LEGAL REQUIREMENTS – Meet DB-HR standards and local regulations.

2. NECESSARY PERMITS – Operating licenses may require acoustic certification.

3. TYPICAL SOLUTIONS – Suspended ceilings, acoustic panels, resilient floors.

4. COST – From €150–250 depending on diagnosis, report and work scope.
